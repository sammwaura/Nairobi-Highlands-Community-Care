about us

our overall goal

The overall goal is to develop KHC to be an icon level 3 Health Centre within Makueni County offering general medical services and specialized services on ophthalmology, orthopedic and Dental medical needs. The facility should also be able to attract medical research expert to use the facility for their studies.

our mission & vission

KHC Services

  • Clinical services i.e;Mother to Child, Family Planning, Immunization, Primary Maternal Services, Normal Deliverly and Post Natal Care, Support to HIV/AIDS clients, TB Clinic, Voluntary Testing, and Counselling
  • Pharmacy services
  • Nursing services
  • Laboratory diagnostic services
  • Health Education and outreach services
